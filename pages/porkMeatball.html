Pork Meat Ball Recipe

The image of the pork meat ball

Pork meat ball is made from ground pork with other seasoning. The Asian version is tasty.

Recipes for making the pork meat ball

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground pork chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • Pepper, coconut soda, and honey
